# How to Make Quote Videos for Instagram Reels

Quote videos are one of the most consistent formats on Instagram Reels. Simple, powerful, and endlessly repeatable — they are the backbone of thousands of pages with millions of followers.

Here is exactly how to make them work.

## The Right Format

Instagram Reels requires **vertical video — 1080×1920 pixels**. This is non-negotiable. Horizontal or square videos lose reach because Instagram deprioritizes non-vertical content in the Reels feed.

Your video should be **15–45 seconds** long. Short enough to watch multiple times (which boosts your play count), long enough to be meaningful.

## What Makes a Quote Video Perform

The best-performing quote videos share these traits:

**1. Text that fills the screen**
Don't use tiny fonts. Your quote should be large, centered, and easy to read in the first second. Viewers scroll fast — if they can't read it immediately, they scroll past.

**2. High contrast**
White text on black backgrounds. Gold text on dark backgrounds. Avoid low-contrast combinations that are hard to read on mobile.

**3. Slow, deliberate reveals**
The best quote videos reveal text line by line. This creates anticipation and keeps viewers watching longer — which tells the algorithm the content is engaging.

**4. Background music that matches the mood**
Cinematic music for stoic quotes. Soft piano for emotional content. Fast beats for motivational content. The music should amplify the quote, not distract from it.

## Consistency Is Everything

One viral video does not build an audience. What builds an audience is posting the same type of content **every single day** until your page becomes associated with that content.
