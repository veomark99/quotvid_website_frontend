# Best Fonts for Quote Videos

Font choice is one of the most underrated decisions in quote video creation. The right font makes your content look professional and trustworthy. The wrong one makes it look cheap — regardless of how good the quote is.

## The Principles of Quote Video Typography

**Readability first.** Your viewer has 1–2 seconds to register your text before deciding to watch or scroll. Choose fonts that are instantly readable at a glance.

**Light weight, bold impact.** Counterintuitively, lighter font weights (300–400) often look more premium for quote content. Heavy fonts can feel aggressive. Thin fonts with generous spacing feel cinematic.

**No decorative fonts for the quote body.** Save decorative or script fonts for your channel name or attribution line only.

## Best English Fonts for Quote Videos

**Montserrat Light** — The industry standard for motivational content. Clean, modern, highly readable. Works at any size.

**Poppins** — Slightly rounder than Montserrat. Great for psychology and wellness content.

**Inter** — Neutral and highly legible. Works across all niches.

**Playfair Display** — Elegant serif. Best for poetry and literary quotes.

## Best Fonts for Arabic and Persian Content

Arabic and Persian quote videos require **Naskh or Nastaliq script fonts** that support right-to-left rendering properly.

**Noto Naskh Arabic** — Clean, modern Arabic font. Works well for motivational and business content.

**Noto Nastaliq Urdu** — The standard for Urdu poetry. Authentic Nastaliq script with proper ligatures.

## Font Sizing

- **Short quotes (under 8 words):** 96px — large and dominant
- **Medium quotes (8–14 words):** 72–82px
- **Long quotes (15–20 words):** 58–68px

Never go below 52px for the main quote body on a 1080×1920 canvas. Anything smaller is unreadable on mobile.

## The Setup Most Creators Use

The most popular setup among viral quote accounts:

- **Quote font:** Montserrat Light, white, centered
- **Background:** Pure black or dark gradient
- **Attribution:** Smaller font, gold or gray color
- **Channel name:** Bottom of frame, subtle opacity
